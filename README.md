# Vijayaba_National_School
VIJAYABA NATIONAL SCHOOL, situated in Hungama is one of the magnificent schools in Sri Lanka. With the 86 years long period of history. It has been able to gain many achievements of excellence both in the academic and non academic fields. VIJAYABA NATIONAL SCHOOL provides education to students from Grade 1 to 13 in Sinhala &amp; English Mediums.
